About Matthew Saidel

Matthew Saidel most recently worked as a field organizer on Cory Booker's successful Senate campaign. He organized a critical swing county to within 900 votes of a victory and developed several young volunteers into inspired activists and future campaign professionals.

He is actively involved in building a data management and analytics skill set, focusing on the Python programming language, various SQL dialects, as well as MongoDB adn NoSQL. He hope to bring these skills to bear on the burgeoning progressive political data community.

He graduated with an MA in International Affairs from the Josef Korbel School of International Studies where he focused on finance, trade, and development. He completed undergraduate coursework in Philosophy (and DJing for college radio) at the George Washington University in 2007.

Contact information and other work experience is available on his resume.
